Prototype

On this page we will show exactly how our app looks and works!

The entire focus on the design is to make it simple, clear and easy to use. Our goal is that everyone can use our app with no issues.

Scroll down to see all the cool stuff you can do with our app!

Start, login and register

The very first page you see when you open our app is the start page. On this page you will be presented with our logo and a short description of our app.

When you click "Let's go" you be loaded into our login screen, where you can login if you have an account or register if it's your first time using the app. To make the registration as simple and quick as possible you have the option to register with an existing facebook or google account.

Add a new product

On this page you can add a new product to your inventory.

Inventory

Here you will see all the items you have scanned into the app. The inventory is split into cold items and dry items. You can see the product name, description and how many days left til expiration.

Recipies

Not sure what to have for dinner? Then the recipies are perfect for you. We show you exactly what dishes you can make with the items you have. We even tell you if your expired items are safe to use with the dish. We have recepies for everything, breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks, smoothies and more!

Schedule

On the "Schedule" page you can plan what dishses to have what days, the app makes sure you have enough of everything, and if you don't it will say what you need and how much.
